You can find in the Brave Athletes Club:

  1. Football

    Football is the most popular game around the world. Football is a team sport based on the principle of cooperation and team spirit. which are among the most important principles of our club. In the Brave Athletes Club, we have developed many training programs for different age groups, which aim to graduate a cohort of skilled football players.

  2. Tennis

    Tennis is an Olympic sport that is played at all levels and groups of society and for all ages. It is a famous and widespread sport all over the world, and the major tennis tournaments enjoy a large public and media following, which places it in an advanced rank among the most important and followed sporting events in the world annually. Therefore, in our club, we have provided special courts for tennis training.

  3. Chess

    Chess is one of the most famous and oldest board games. Which aims to strengthen concentration, as it requires great concentration in every step a person takes. Chess also activates and strengthens the brain, as it works to develop the ability to recognize patterns and make decisions.
